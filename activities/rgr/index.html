Pack Event: Raingutter Regatta

By Cubmaster

Saturday, Nov 16th 2024!

Register here for participating. If you have participated in voting in Den channel, this is not required.

How to prepare ?

This blog is a good starting point and you may explore other resources for information that falls within the recommended BSA cubscouts standards in preparing the boat for the racing.

Racing Groups & Prizes

  • LIONs/TIGERs
  • Wolf/Bears
  • Webelos/AoL

There will be 1st, 2nd, 3rd places per group and participation recognition for each participant!

Siblings & Friends

Siblings can participate at the end of th race using the cubscout boat OR come prepared on their own from the supplies available at the scoutshop! Invite your scouts friends and families with RSVP so that we can have a head count for the arrangements.

Overall Plan

To make this event happen and joyful for the cubscouts we need help from each one of the adult leaders in the pack. Please help by reachingout to the co-ordinator at best of your availability.
