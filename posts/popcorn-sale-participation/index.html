Message from Pack Committee on Popcorn Sale

Assalamualaikum Pack499!,

Alhamdulillah, it was great to see many Cubscouts showed-up today to kickstart the next scouting year.

There is a lot of excitement about the Popcorn Sale. The event will be beneficial and successful with your participation, inshaAllah.

Please note while the event brings excitement, it comes with considerable background planning in advance and the efforts continue until the sale concludes. The first step in making it successful is through a near accurate forecast of upcoming year (2025-26) returning scouts and their commitment. We had sent you the intent to return / registration forms for responses in June 2025 for the same. JazakumAllahuKhair for those who responded. We stil have pending responses. Please complete them before 24th Aug 2024. The responses are crucial for us to plan for the year and importantly for the Popcorn Sale event. It involves financial responsibility with liability to payback the order invoiced amount with an exception to return only 10% of un-sold inventory.

The current orders are made based on responses we had received. Looking at the excitement, we will run short of inventory very soon. Help us with responses to confirm your availability and commitment to continue with scouting this year. It will help us to revise the orders to have enough supply for the successful event, inshaAllah.

Please note, Pack policy is to encourage participation through incentivising. This year, scouts can avail 100% of their earned commission towards their registration renewal fee with 0% withholding towards the pack funds!

Our unit commission is 25% as of now and may improve based on everyone’s participation and achieving the minimum target ($250 sale per scout). To earn $100 towards the registration fee on their own, scout to sell $400 worth of popcorn with < 10% unsold committed inventory.

A simple example:

  • Say, a Cubscout (registered and paid pack dues) commits $450 sale after registering in trails-end app.
  • Cubscout will be provided with $450 worth of inventory.
  • Cubscout claims store front reservation OR do wagon OR online sale upto $450 or $450 - $45 (10%) = $405 sale with $45 inventory return to earn 25% commission of the sale.
  • Sale proceedings goes to the council and gets transferred to Pack after the sale concludes in Nov 2025.
  • Scouts can use the earned commission towards their registration renewal, usually due in Dec 2025. We can work with the scouts having the registration dates slightly different to ensure the earnigns are applied towards their scouting renewal payments.
  • Surplus comission is held in pack account can be claimed towards their adventures after a nominal contribution towards the packfunds to improve the scouting experience.
  • We will govern the storefront hours claims to help everyone gets a fair chance without unncessary/excessive holding by a single/few scouts with your help to bring to our observation.
  • We suggest no more than two slots (4 hrs) holding at any given time seen from the trails-end app.

For further collaboration on this event, check https://chat.sbiapack499.org/pack499/channels/popcorn-sale channel.

Thank you for your co-operation,

~ Pack Committee
