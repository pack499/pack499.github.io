Raingutter Regatta

The raingutter regatta is a racing event for Cub Scouts in the Boy Scouts of America that is the sailboat equivalent of the pinewood derby.

Service Projects

A service project focused on orphans can encompass various initiatives aimed at improving the lives of children who have lost their parents or guardians, often with a focus on education, healthcare, and overall well-being

Pack Events

a variety of activities that bring together dens and families for a fun and educational experience. These events include monthly pack meetings, special activities like Pinewood Derby and Blue and Gold Banquets, and various community service projects.

Outdoor Events

Scouting involves a wide array of outdoor events and activities, from day hikes and campouts to high adventure expeditions and national jamborees. These activities aim to develop skills, foster leadership, and promote citizenship while enjoying the outdoors.

Pinewood Derby

Since it was first introduced in 1953 by Don Murphy, a Cubmaster in California, Pine Wood Derby(PWD) has become one of the most popular and favorite activity for cubscouts. It is one of the most celebrated tradition in Cubscouts

Family Campouts

Family camping offers a unique opportunity to create lasting memories, strengthen bonds, and experience the joys of nature together
